# AutoAssess AI - Intelligent RAG Assessment Platform
Welcome to AutoAssess AI! Ever wished you could hand a stack of heavy PDF documents to an AI, ask it complex assessment questions, and get accurate answers without the AI making things up? That’s exactly what I built here.
Using the power of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), this platform ingests your specific documents, builds a private knowledge base, and acts as an expert assistant that grounds every single answer in your actual text.
No hallucinations. Just smart, context-aware answers.
